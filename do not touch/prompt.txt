Trippy Mates is not a traditional travel agency.

The platform combines:

1. Travel Packages
2. Local Captains
3. Travel Communities
4. Custom Trip Planning

The primary USP is:

BOOK A CAPTAIN

A Captain is a verified local travel expert who can:

* Physically accompany travelers
* Provide virtual guidance via call/video call
* Recommend local attractions
* Help with language barriers
* Offer emergency support
* Create personalized experiences
